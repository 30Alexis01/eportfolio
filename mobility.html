Mobility objective

I aim to complete a six-month international internship in software engineering, starting in 2027, in order to experience an English-speaking professional environment and strengthen my technical skills.

Target countries

  • Secondary option: United States

Professional motivation

I am particularly interested in software applications related to drones and systems with concrete real-world use, especially in the fields of rescue operations and civil safety. Australia is especially attractive to me because of projects such as those developed by Little Ripper Group, which designs rescue drones used in coastal environments.

My primary focus remains software development, while being aware that drones also rely on embedded systems. In this context, I am open to orienting my academic path towards embedded systems if this proves relevant for such projects.
