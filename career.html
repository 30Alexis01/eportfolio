Overview

I aim to work in software development on concrete projects where software is used in real-world situations. I am particularly interested in software with a clear purpose, especially in contexts related to emergency response, drones, aeronautics, or more broadly, complex technical systems.

My profile

  • Education: ENSEEIHT (Toulouse INP) – Software Engineering
  • Strengths: rigor, autonomy, ability to work under constraints, teamwork
  • Interests: applied software, emergency response, sport

Criteria and choices

  • Projects with a strong technical and applied dimension
  • Software integrated into real systems
  • Collaborative and team-oriented work environments
  • Opportunities for international exposure

Professional objectives

  • Short term: complete a software development internship, ideally abroad, in order to gain industry experience.
  • Medium term: graduate as a software engineering engineer with solid initial professional experience.
  • Long term: work on software projects related to emergency response or drones, or, depending on opportunities, in the aeronautics sector.

Target roles

  • Software engineer – development-oriented
  • Software engineer – embedded systems
